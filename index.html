About Me

Hey there!
I'm a full-stack software developer who enjoys building software that's both practical and enjoyable to use. I first got into programming in grade 10 after writing my first "Hello, World!" program in Python, and I've been hooked on problem-solving ever since.

I graduated from the Computer Programming & Analysis program at Fanshawe College, where I built a strong foundation in software development, databases, and application design. Since then, I've continued expanding my skills through hands-on projects and by exploring new technologies whenever I can.

Growing up in rural Southwestern Ontario helped shape the way I approach work and learning. What I enjoy most about programming is the process of turning ideas into something real: breaking down problems, designing solutions, and continuously improving them over time. I'm especially interested in full-stack development because it allows me to work across both the technical and user-facing sides of an application.

Outside of coding, I enjoy puzzles, photography, and anything that challenges me to think creatively or analytically.

If you'd like to learn more about my background and projects, check out the Experience section — or feel free to reach out.
